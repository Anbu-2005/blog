About Us

Kongunadu Polytechnic College is committed to producing dynamic and disciplined technocrats through our value-added, need-based, cost-effective technical education, and appropriate short-term training programs. Our aim is to become an Institute of Excellence in providing full-time diploma courses.

Our vision is to emerge as a world-class institution in imparting necessary technical skills and English communication skills, and thus offering quality education to downtrodden students.

Infrastructure

Our campus is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including modern laboratories, libraries, and recreational areas, to ensure a conducive learning environment for our students.

Achievements

Over the years, Kongunadu Polytechnic College has received numerous accolades for its outstanding contributions to technical education and student development.

About III Cell

INDUSTRY INSTITUTE INTERACTION CELL mainly operates in our college to reduce the gap between the institute and the industry. This cell will provide a great industrial exposure to our diploma students and subsequent job opportunities of our young engineers in industries across the country. The main function of this cell is to provide career opportunities to the final year students through campus placement programme. Great and closer collaboration with the industries is our of the unique features of this cell. The main motto of this cell is to place our final year students in reputed core and software industries. The above goal is achieved through faculty industry training program, career guidance program, value-added courses, & quality internship training programs.

Vision

To provide career opportunities to the students in all over India through Campus Placement Programme by equipping the students with relevant technical skills.

Mission

To provide career with higher studies opportunities to the Students in all over the world through Campus Placement Programme by equipping the students with relevant technical skill & industry expected skills.
